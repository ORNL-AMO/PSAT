Training

PSAT workshops are available. A listing of upcoming training events is available at the DOE web site:

http://www1.eere.energy.gov/industry/bestpractices/events_calendar.asp

The training includes both end-user and specialist workshops. Those attending the specialist workshops who take and successfully complete an exam given at the end are designated as "PSAT Specialist". A listing of PSAT specialists is also available on the DOE web site at:

http://www1.eere.energy.gov/industry/bestpractices/psat.html
